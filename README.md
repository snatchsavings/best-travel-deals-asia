## 💡 How To Save Maximum On Travel

**Step 1 — Always Log In Before Searching**
Most platforms show lower member prices only 
to logged-in users. Always sign in before 
comparing prices.

**Step 2 — Use Your Bank Card Strategically**
Bank card deals are contractual — they work 
far more reliably than random promo codes. 
Check which card gives the best deal on your 
chosen platform before paying.

**Step 3 — Check Deals Before You Search**
Find your verified promo code first — then 
go to the booking platform. Never search for 
codes at checkout under time pressure.

**Step 4 — Stack Discounts Where Possible**
Promo code + bank card cashback + member price 
can all apply together on some platforms. 
Always apply promo code first, then select 
payment method.

**Step 5 — Book On The Right Day**
Weekend flash deals and payday offers refresh 
regularly. Friday is consistently the best day 
for Klook deals. Monday mornings often have 
the freshest Agoda prices.
